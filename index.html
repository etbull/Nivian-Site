Australian privacy laws, including the Privacy Act 1988, require businesses to protect personal information. Hosting your AI in Australia ensures compliance with these regulations, keeps your data within Australian legal jurisdiction, and protects against foreign access requests. For industries like healthcare and legal services, this is often mandatory.

About Nivian

Nivian helps Australian businesses adopt AI safely, privately, and with complete confidence.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make AI accessible and compliant for all. There are very real security risks with inputting sensitive data to public AI systems. We empower firms to use AI without sacrificing privacy, security, or regulatory requirements.

Our Approach

Practical, simple solutions, built around your workflows. We integrate AI directly into your tools and documents so it actually improves daily operations, not complicates them. No over-engineering or complex interfaces.

Our Values

Clarity. Security. Humility. Every system we build should be simple and able to be understood by anyone while also maintaining the highest privacy standards. We also keep things grounded and honest, no ego, no jargon.
